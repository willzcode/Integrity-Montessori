Welcome to Integrity Montessori School, where we inspire a love for learning and nurture each child’s potential in a safe, inclusive environment. About the School

Integrity Montessori School, established in 2016, is dedicated to providing a nurturing, child-centered education based on the Montessori philosophy. We focus on fostering academic excellence, independence, and character development, allowing each child to learn and grow at their own pace. Our experienced educators create a supportive environment where students develop critical thinking, emotional intelligence, and a strong sense of responsibility, preparing them to thrive both academically and personally. At Integrity Montessori, we are committed to shaping well-rounded, compassionate individuals ready to make a positive impact on the world.

Mission

To deliver high-quality education and childcare that fosters social, emotional, physical, and cognitive growth in a safe, respectful, and inclusive setting, laying the foundation for lifelong learning.

Vision

To nurture confident, well-rounded individuals who achieve their full potential in a welcoming, safe, and supportive environment where everyone is valued and achievements are celebrated.

Outstanding Curricular Offerings

Our curriculum is designed to nurture students' academic, social, and emotional growth. Delivered by dedicated and inspiring educators, we ensure that every pupil reaches their full potential, both during their time with us and beyond. We believe in the importance of a positive attitude toward learning and encourage students to embrace challenges, collaborate with others, and develop independence.

Creche and Nursery

Our Creche and Nursery programs focus on providing a safe and nurturing environment where young children begin their learning journey. Through play-based learning, we foster social development and build foundational skills that set the stage for future success.

Kindergarten

The goal of our Kindergarten program is to ensure that every child can read and write by the end of the year. With a team of qualified teachers, we provide a strong academic foundation that equips each child with the necessary skills to continue thriving in their educational path.

Primary

Our Primary program emphasizes the development of key academic skills, critical thinking, and personal growth. We foster a supportive learning environment that encourages curiosity, resilience, and a lifelong love of learning, preparing students for success in their academic and personal lives.
